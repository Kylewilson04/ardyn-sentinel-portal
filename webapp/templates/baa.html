BUSINESS ASSOCIATE AGREEMENT

This Business Associate Agreement ("BAA") is entered into between the Covered Entity and Ardyn Intelligence ("Business Associate") to ensure compliance with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) and its implementing regulations.

1. PERMITTED USES AND DISCLOSURES

Business Associate may use or disclose Protected Health Information (PHI) only as permitted by this Agreement or as Required by Law. Business Associate shall not use or disclose PHI in any manner that would violate Subpart E of 45 CFR Part 164 if done by Covered Entity.

2. SAFEGUARDS

Business Associate shall use appropriate safeguards to prevent use or disclosure of PHI other than as provided for by this Agreement. Business Associate implements Technical Safeguards including encryption, access controls, and audit logging.

3. REPORTING

Business Associate shall report to Covered Entity any use or disclosure of PHI not provided for by this Agreement of which Business Associate becomes aware.

4. SUBCONTRACTORS

Business Associate shall ensure that any subcontractors agree to the same restrictions and conditions that apply to Business Associate with respect to PHI.

5. ACCESS AND AMENDMENT

Business Associate shall provide access to PHI in a Designated Record Set and shall amend PHI as required by 45 CFR § 164.526.

6. AUDIT CONTROLS

Business Associate shall make internal practices, books, and records relating to the use and disclosure of PHI available to Covered Entity for purposes of determining compliance with this Agreement.

7. RETURN OR DESTRUCTION OF PHI

Upon termination of this Agreement, Business Associate shall return or destroy all PHI received from Covered Entity.

8. TERMINATION

Covered Entity may terminate this Agreement if Business Associate violates a material term of this Agreement.
